Programme "Advances in Microscopy" 2017-2018

Info
  • The lectures are scheduled on Wednesdays (between 09.00 and 12.00) on Campus Gasthuisberg, starting on October 4th 2017. The course is part of the Master Biomedical Sciences, but is open to PhD's students, postdocs and technical staff of any lab that uses high end microscopy.
Room change
  • The majority of the classes will be held in the new teaching tower (white building), room ALO 07.200 (two lectures will be in Pentalfa).
