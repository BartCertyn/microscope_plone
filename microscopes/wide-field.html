Olympus fluorescence microscope


  • The Olympus microscope can be used for viewing fluorescence or brightfield images and capturing them on the XM10 or SC30 cameras respectively.
  • You need to be trained, 4 people at the time, ~ once a month (Training+Access: 100 € pp for TARGID members; 150 € pp for external). Request training here.
  • For people not belonging to TARGID, a 10 €/hr usage fee will be charged.
  • Please be aware:
    • Use the booking sheet to book the microscope
    • Cover the microscope and turn off the computer if you are the last user.
    • Please fill in the user log.
  • Person responsible: Youcef Kazwiny.
